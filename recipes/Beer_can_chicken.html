ingrediates

  • 1/4 cup gralic powder

  • Coarse black pepper

Instructions

  1. Removed inards and clean chicken
  2. season chicken with seasoning mixture and place upright on beer can
  3. Preheat grill for 375 degrees and put on Chicken
  4. Cook till internal temperature 165 degrees(about 1 1/2 hours)
  5. Let rest 15 minutes and enjoy
