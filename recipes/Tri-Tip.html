Ingrediants

  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 medium Lime
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 1/2B pound Tri Tip

Instructions

  1. Combine ingrediates for marinade
  2. Liberally marinade Tri Tip for at least 8 hours refrigerated
  3. Preheat grill to 225 degrees
  4. Cook to an internal temperature of 135 degrees and remove from grill
  5. let rest 15 minutes, cut, serve, and enjoy
